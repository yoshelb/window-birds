Window Birds

For months I only had one type of bird coming to this little bird feeder. A few weeks ago as it got colder a bunch of other kinds of birds started arriving. I found out that as it gets cold birds who live further north or in forests start to turn up at backyard feeders to eat the readily availavle and high fat nuts.Learn more about these birdies.
